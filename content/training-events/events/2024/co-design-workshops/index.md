The Australian Research Data Commons (ARDC) invited the research community to 2 open co-design workshops to better understand the challenges around establishing the Language Data Commons of Australia (LDaCA). The sessions aimed to learn more about:

- what outcomes and developments would be of most benefit
- what will be both valuable and feasible
- how ARDC investments can align with other activity in the sector.

*Presenters:*

- Jenny Fewster, Director, HASS and Indigenous Research Data Commons, ARDC
- Professor Michael Haugh, University of Queensland
- Robert McLellan, University of Queensland
