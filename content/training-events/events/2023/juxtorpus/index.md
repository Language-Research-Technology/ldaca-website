This hybrid taster webinar introduced the latest addition to the suite of Australian Text Analytics Platform (<GlossaryLink display="ATAP" id="atap" />) text analysis tools — Juxtorpus. Developed to provide a unified framework for managing and exploring text contents and <GlossaryLink display="metadata" id="metadata" />, Juxtorpus offers a Corpus package that enables flexible building, exploration and slicing of your corpus while maintaining its shape, and a Jux package that allows for easy comparison and highlighting of differences between any two corpora with tools and visualisations that come off-the-shelf. During the webinar, we also demonstrated how to use other <GlossaryLink display="ATAP" id="atap" /> tools in combination with the Corpus to create a reusable workflow that boosts your analysis capabilities.

**Length**: 90 minutes

**Leaders**: Chao Sun
