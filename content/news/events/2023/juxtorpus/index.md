Join us for a hybrid taster webinar on the latest addition to the suite of Australian Text Analytics Platform (<GlossaryLink display="ATAP" id="atap" />) text analysis tools — Juxtorpus. Developed to provide a unified framework for managing and exploring text contents and <GlossaryLink display="metadata" id="metadata" />, Juxtorpus offers a Corpus package that enables flexible building, exploration and slicing of your corpus while maintaining its shape, and a Jux package that allows for easy comparison and highlighting of differences between any two corpora with tools and visualisations that come off-the-shelf. During the webinar, we'll also show you how to use other <GlossaryLink display="ATAP" id="atap" /> tools in combination with the Corpus to create a reusable workflow that will boost your analysis capabilities.

This 1.5-hour webinar will come with minimal hands-on opportunities, and we invite anyone interested in learning how to handle and analyse their digital text collections to join us. No programming knowledge or skills are required.

**Length**: 90 minutes

**Leaders**: Chao Sun
